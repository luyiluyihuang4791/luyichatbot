To encourage those excellent students and to help students in financial straits, LTU offers more than NT$30,000,000 scholarships each year. Of all scholarships, "Excellent Student Scholarship in Entrance Examination" is offered to LTU freshmen who are with excellent academic merits in entrance examination, "Excellent Foreign Students Scholarship" is founded especially for foreign students’ tuition fee which is totally deducted for foreign students with excellent academic records.

*For undergraduate program: high school graduates;
*For graduate programs: college graduate with B.A. degree; proficiency in Chinese;
*Certificate of financial support
