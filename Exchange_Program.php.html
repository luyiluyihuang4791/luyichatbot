1.International students are defined as those who are neither citizens of the Republic of China(*), nor Overseas Chinese. Those who have had their R.O.C. nationality annulled for 8 years or more are eligible to apply. This regulation does not apply to students of Hong Kong or Macao residency.
2.International students who graduated from a high school, college or university recognized by the Ministry of Education in the R.O.C. can apply for the admission (please refer to the website http://www.edu.te/). International students with a high school diploma are eligible to apply for undergraduate programs; bachelor's degree holders for Master's programs; Master's degree holders for doctoral programs.

*According to Article 2 of the Law of Nationality, a person who meets one of the following requirements acquires the nationality of the Republic of China:
(1)a person whose father or mother was, at the time of his/her birth, a citizen of the Republic of China.
(2)a person born after the death of his/her father or mother who was, at the time of his/her death, a citizen of the Republic of China.
(3)a person born in the territory of the Republic of China and whose parents are both unknown or are stateless.
(4)a naturalized person.
The previous specified in sub-paragraphs (1)and (2)of the proceding paragraph shall apply to minors on the date of the promulgation of the amendment of his law.
